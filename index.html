04 — Competitors


For Growers

Submit your work.

Saturday · Flower Cup

Enter your flower.

Open to growers across the spectrum — homegrowers, caregivers, licensed cultivators. Submit your strongest jar and let a room full of peers tell you what they think.

Sunday · Extract Cup

Enter your extracts.

Solventless, hydrocarbon, distillate — all welcome at the right altitude. Bring the work that represents your bench at its best, and let it speak for itself.

How it works

The format in plain language.

01

Submit your jar

Flower Cup: 1–2oz of your finest, a quart mason jar's worth. Extract Cup: 10g of your bench's best. We can't promise to return the unused portion — submit what you'd be proud to share.

Pro tip — more bud, more peers tasting it
02

Check in early

Flower competitors: Friday's VIP party from 3pm (preferred), or Saturday between 10–11am. Extract competitors: Sunday between 10–11am. Don't be early. Don't be late.

03

Numbered, sealed, scanned

Each entry is moved into a numbered mason jar — your number stays secret until tasting wraps. Humidity packs welcome on flower. Every jar is swept by the Nasal Ranger for terp enhancement spray. Offenders get publicly named.

04

Cast your tokens

Judges receive a glass one-hitter, a lighter, and JR Tokin voting tokens — five for the Flower Cup, three for Extract. Drop tokens into each entry's Recharge bag. Most tokens wins. No cheating.

Competitor Information

How to compete.

Two days. Two cups. Bring your strongest work and let a room full of peers tell you what they think.

Saturday · June 6 · 12–5pm

Flower Cup

How to Enter
  1. Purchase a Saturday Flower Cup competitor ticket.
  2. Complete the competitor entry form — link sent to your email prior to event.
  3. Submit your entry at the event.
Entry Requirements 1–2oz finest flower

A quart mason jar's worth.

Pro TipThe more bud you submit, the more people will be able to judge it.

We cannot guarantee that we will be able to return the unused portion (if any) of your entry.

Competition Check-In

VIP Party Friday — preferredCheck in no earlier than 3pm at the venue. If you plan on attending the VIP Party, make sure you've purchased a VIP Party ticket.

SaturdayCheck in no earlier than 10am at the venue. Check-in ends at 11am sharp.

Handling

Your entry will be transferred to one of our 1-quart mason jars, lidded and numbered.

You're welcome to include a humidity pack with your entry, but it is not required.

All entries will be scanned by Nasal Ranger to detect the presence of any terp enhancement sprays. Violators will be shamed publicly and repeatedly.

Your jar number remains secret until the conclusion of tasting.

Tasting & Voting

Competition judging runs from 12–5pm.

All judges receive a glass one-hitter and lighter. Budtenders serve samples in the Main Tent.

Each judge receives five JR Tokin voting tokens. Each entry has an associated Recharge bag — drop one or more tokens into the bag to vote for your favorite entries.

Rules
  • Don't be early.
  • Don't be late.
  • The entry that collects the most JR Tokin voting tokens wins.
  • No cheating.
